Super Mario Sunshine

The most charming Mario game ever. It has the best concepts in a Mario game!

Angelo's travel tips:

1. Appreciate the one of the best Mario locations ever made by using the
flashiest moveset Mario has ever had in the jungle gym-style parts of the game.
2. Instantly get refreshed not just by the sights, but also this game's
phenomenal cutscenes.
3.Take a sun-drenched vacation from traditional Super Mario course themes
by visiting rural areas filled with fun characters to talk to.
