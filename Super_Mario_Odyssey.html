Super Mario Odyssey

This game. This is my favorite out of them all. It's what got me into the franchise.

Angelo's travel tips:

1. Enjoy the vast open worlds with tons to see and tons to do. Be sure to check out the
ironclad ancient Steam Gardens, and the bustling New Donk City.
2. Practice controlling the smoothest Mario moveset and reach new places.
3. Read the charming character dialogue and get immersed in the world and its stories.
